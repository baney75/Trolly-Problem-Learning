## Philosophy Concepts Covered

### Consequentialism
- **Act-Consequentialism**: Evaluate each action by its consequences
- **Rule-Consequentialism**: Follow rules that produce good outcomes
- **Utilitarianism**: Maximize total happiness (Bentham, Mill)
- **Hedonic Calculus**: Quantifying pleasure and pain

### Deontology (Kantian Ethics)
- **Categorical Imperative** - Three Formulations:
  1. Universal Law: Act only on maxims you can will as universal law
  2. Humanity Formula: Treat persons as ends, never merely as means
  3. Kingdom of Ends: Act as if your maxim legislates for all rational beings
- **Good Will**: Acting from duty, not inclination
- **Hypothetical vs. Categorical Imperatives**

### Moral Dilemmas
- **Lever Scenario**: Active vs. passive intervention
- **Footbridge Scenario**: Personal involvement and instrumentalization
- **Loop Scenario**: Indirect harm as means
